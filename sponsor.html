Sponsor the Misfits

We are currently seeking sponsorship and would love to partner with individuals and companies who share our vision of promoting sport in our community.

Our Program

Our basketball program aims to provide a constructive and engaging outlet for local female youth, offering them the opportunity to develop valuable life skills such as teamwork, leadership, and discipline, while promoting a healthy and active lifestyle. By sponsoring our program, your company would have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on their lives and contribute to the overall well-being of our community.

What’s in it for you?

As a sponsor, you would enjoy several benefits that would boost your company and show your dedication to corporate social responsibility:

  • Brand Exposure: Your company logo featured on our team gear, promotional materials, and at our events, reaching out to the local community and increasing your brand awareness.
  • Publicity Opportunities: We would acknowledge your company as a sponsor in our social media posts and website, attracting a large audience and generating positive publicity.
  • Positive Brand Association: By partnering with a program that supports youth development and community engagement, you can showcase your commitment to making a difference.

Your Contribution

As a sponsor, your funds will go towards our 2024 Basketball Saskatchewan Inc. membership, gym facility fees, tournament fees, team building events, and team apparel. We would be happy to discuss the specific sponsorship levels and benefits in more detail and we are open to tailoring a sponsorship package that aligns with your company's goals and objectives.
